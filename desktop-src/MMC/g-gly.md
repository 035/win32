<span id="mmc_guid_globally_unique_identifier__gly"></span><span id="MMC_GUID_GLOBALLY_UNIQUE_IDENTIFIER__GLY"></span>**GUID (globally unique identifier)**
</dt> <dd>

For COM. A 16-byte (128-bit) value that uniquely identifies some entity, such as a snap-in's CLSID or the node type of a scope or result item. A GUID is also referred to as a universally unique identifier (UUID).
