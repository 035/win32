-   [**GetValue**](/previous-versions/windows/desktop/api/Pla/nf-pla-idatacollectorset-getvalue)

-   [**SetCredentials**](/previous-versions/windows/desktop/api/Pla/nf-pla-idatacollectorset-setcredentials)
-   [**SetValue**](/previous-versions/windows/desktop/api/Pla/nf-pla-idatacollectorset-setvalue)
-   [**SetXml**](/previous-versions/windows/desktop/api/Pla/nf-pla-idatacollectorset-setxml)
-   [**Start**](/previous-versions/windows/desktop/api/Pla/nf-pla-idatacollectorset-start)
-   [**Stop**](/previous-versions/windows/desktop/api/Pla/nf-pla-idatacollectorset-stop)
