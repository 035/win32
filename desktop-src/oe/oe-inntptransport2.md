# INNTPTransport2 interface

\[**INNTPTransport2** is available for use in the operating systems specified in the Requirements section. It may be altered or unavailable in subsequent versions.\]

Extends [**INNTPTransport**](oe-inntptransport.md) to control timeouts and refine asynchronous behavior.

## Members

The **INNTPTransport2** interface inherits from [**INNTPTransport**](oe-inntptransport.md) but does not have additional members.
