# IMimeMessageW interface

Do not use. Creates and manipulates messages. The methods of **IMimeMessageW** support Unicode.

## Members

The **IMimeMessageW** interface inherits from [**IMimeMessage**](oe-imimemessage.md) but does not have additional members.
