# ITTSAttributesW

\[Microsoft Agent is deprecated as of Windows 7, and may be unavailable in subsequent versions of Windows.\]

The engine must support pitch and speed attributes through the [**PitchSet**](lwef-pitchset_method), [**PitchGet**](lwef-pitchget_method), [**SpeedSet**](lwef-speedset_method), and [**SpeedGet**](lwef-speedget_method) methods.
