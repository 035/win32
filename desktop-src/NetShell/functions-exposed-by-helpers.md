-   [**NS\_CONTEXT\_DUMP\_FN**](ns-context-dump-fn.md) (Dump function)
-   [**NS\_DLL\_STOP\_FN**](ns-dll-stop-fn.md) (DLL Stop function)
-   [**NS\_HELPER\_START\_FN**](ns-helper-start-fn.md) (Start function)
-   [**NS\_HELPER\_STOP\_FN**](ns-helper-stop-fn.md) (Stop function)
