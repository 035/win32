# WSD Provider PKEYs

\[Function Discovery is available for use in the operating systems specified in the Requirements section. It may be altered or unavailable in subsequent versions.\]

WSD services and devices are quite frequently discovered by the PnP-X Provider. WSD devices must support many of the PnP-X PKEYs. For more information, see [**PnP-X Provider PKEYs**](pnp-x-provider-pkeys.md).
