| [**UI\_ANIMATION\_SLOPE**](/windows/win32/UIAnimation/ne-uianimation-__midl___midl_itf_uianimation_0000_0009_0001?branch=master)<br/>                               | Defines animation slope characteristics.<br/>                                                                                                  |
