<span id="wmi_v2.gloss_namespace"></span><span id="WMI_V2.GLOSS_NAMESPACE"></span>**namespace**
</dt> <dd>

A categorization of classes and instances used to control their scope and visibility. Namespaces are not physical locations. They are more like logical databases containing specific classes and instances. Namespaces are represented by the [**\_\_Namespace**](https://msdn.microsoft.com/library/aa394657) system class or a class derived from it.
