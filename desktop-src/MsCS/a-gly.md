<dl> <dt>

<span id="_wolf_active_gly"></span><span id="_WOLF_ACTIVE_GLY"></span>**active**
</dt> <dd>

A [*node*](https://www.bing.com/search?q=*node*) that is running and participating in cluster operations.

</dd> <dt>

<span id="_wolf_arbitration_gly"></span><span id="_WOLF_ARBITRATION_GLY"></span>**arbitration**
</dt> <dd>

Process of obtaining ownership of the [*quorum resource*](https://www.bing.com/search?q=*quorum resource*). A quorum resource implements the [**Arbitrate**](/previous-versions/windows/desktop/api/ResApi/nc-resapi-parbitrate_routine) and [**Release**](/previous-versions/windows/desktop/api/ResApi/nc-resapi-prelease_routine) entry point functions in its [*resource DLL*](https://www.bing.com/search?q=*resource DLL*) to support arbitration.

</dd> <dt>

<span id="_wolf_available_disk_gly"></span><span id="_WOLF_AVAILABLE_DISK_GLY"></span>**available disk**
</dt> <dd>

A [*cluster-capable disk*](https://www.bing.com/search?q=*cluster-capable disk*) that is not associated with any existing [*Physical Disk resource*](https://www.bing.com/search?q=*Physical Disk resource*) and that is not being used as the [*quorum resource*](https://www.bing.com/search?q=*quorum resource*).

</dd> </dl>
