<dl> <dt>

<span id="_wolf_active_gly"></span><span id="_WOLF_ACTIVE_GLY"></span>**active**
</dt> <dd>

A [*node*](n-gly.md#-wolf-node-gly) that is running and participating in cluster operations.

</dd> <dt>

<span id="_wolf_arbitration_gly"></span><span id="_WOLF_ARBITRATION_GLY"></span>**arbitration**
</dt> <dd>

Process of obtaining ownership of the [*quorum resource*](q-gly.md#-wolf-quorum-resource-gly). A quorum resource implements the [**Arbitrate**](arbitrate.md) and [**Release**](release.md) entry point functions in its [*resource DLL*](r-gly.md#-wolf-resource-dll-gly) to support arbitration.

</dd> <dt>

<span id="_wolf_available_disk_gly"></span><span id="_WOLF_AVAILABLE_DISK_GLY"></span>**available disk**
</dt> <dd>

A [*cluster-capable disk*](c-gly.md#-wolf-cluster-capable-disk-gly) that is not associated with any existing [*Physical Disk resource*](p-gly.md#-wolf-physical-disk-resource-gly) and that is not being used as the [*quorum resource*](q-gly.md#-wolf-quorum-resource-gly).

</dd> </dl>
