# To test changes in the background or foreground color of a pop-up window

1.  Open a project (.hhp) file, click the **Test** menu, and then click **Popup Attributes**.
2.  Use the **Margin**, **Color**, and **Font** boxes to test changes to the appearance of your text-only pop-up windows.
3.  Click **Test**, and then click **Done**.
